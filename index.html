Recommended Guidelines for Dean's Office Funded OTPS and Meal Thresholds

⚠ Draft — April 2026 Project UR006053
Project UR006053 should primarily be used for expenses related to Instructional or Academic support. Supporting documentation and/or a valid business purpose must be provided for all charges in order to receive Dean's Office approval.

Recruitment, Lecturers & Speakers — Meal Policy
Category Max / Person Person Limit Notes

Requesting Funds Over the Above Limits
  • Chair submits written request to the Dean with clear justification
  • Must include: name of recruit/speaker, event date, all attendee names
✓ Allowable Charges
Student Related
  • Graduation
  • Orientation
  • Info sessions for prospective/incoming students; recruitment expenses
  • Seminars and/or guest speakers
✓ Allowable Charges
Office / Admin Related
→ KEY SUPPLIERS
  • General departmental office supplies (copier, paper, toners, pens, pencils, flash drives)
  • Pantry supplies (coffee, paper goods, water — excludes snacks, soda, meals)
  • Laptops/computers for administrators (per SEAS laptop policy)
  • Furniture for new or existing staff (prior Dean's approval + justification required)
  • Professional conferences for job-related skills (HR approved — e.g. NACURA, SRI, NACUBO)
Note

Pantry supplies ≠ meals. Coffee and paper goods are fine. Lunch and snacks for the team? That's on the department — not this budget.

✓ Allowable Charges
Faculty Related
  • Instructional & office supplies; laptop (per SEAS laptop policy)
  • Recruitment expenses (must use initiative 29204) including candidate travel & onboarding (e.g. office furniture)
  • Adjunct professor expenses
✓ Allowable Charges
Department Chair
  • Any expenses related to the Chair appointment with appropriate supporting documentation
✓ Allowable Charges
Meetings & Events
  • Faculty meetings
  • Curriculum meetings
  • Monthly admin meetings
  • Employee retirement / farewell events
✕ Restrictions
Not Allowable on UR006053
  • Promotional items
  • Awards & prizes
  • Christmas parties or non-academic social events (prior Dean's Office approval required)
Golden Rule: If the expense serves an instructional or academic purpose, start here. If it's social, promotional, or purely departmental morale — it likely doesn't belong on UR006053. When in doubt, get Dean's Office approval in writing first.
Important Notes
Student Social Clubs / Events Dean's Office recommends individual Social Club Departmental projects. Budget is separated from UR006053 OTPS — total budget is unchanged but OTPS is reduced by the allocated amount.
Faculty Discretionary Funds Can be used for student support activities. Cannot cover non-CU business expenses.
Gift Funds Restricted strictly to expenses stipulated in the donor's term sheet. No exceptions.
